Research Interests

My research lies primiary in L2 phonetics/phonology, perception-production link, nonnative Human-AI interaction.

  1. L2 phonetics/phonology: One of my main research interests focus on speech adaptation happening between native and nonnative speakers of English, e.g., Japanese-English and Mandarin-English L2 speakers, particulary their use of phonetic cues disambiguiting English tense-lax vowels
    • ASA Poster: Zhang et al (2023) – Speech adaptation in conversation: Effects of segment cue-weighting strategy for non-native speakers
    • New Sounds: Zhang et al(2025), Native-nonnative speech adaptations in conversation: Effects of L1 background

  2. Sperception-production link: I am interested in how speech perception and production link is estiblished between native-nonnative interaction.

  3. Nonnative Human-AI interaction: I am interested in whether AI-nonnaitve interaction would be an effecitve tool under interactive setting.

  4. Brain vs Bahavior testing during interaction: I am interested in examineing interaction using both behavior and neuroimaging methods
