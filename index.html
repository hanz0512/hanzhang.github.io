About Me

I am a PhD Student in the Department of Linguistics at Simon Fraser University

My primary research interests focus on speech of second language speakers. Specifically, I study how L2 speakers of English with different L1 background — such as native speakers of Japanese and Mandarin Chinese, communicate with native speakers of English tnrough unconsicous phonetic adaptation, and in which aspect of speech acoustics they are perceived to be "nonnative". Using experimental methods, I aim to examine those speech dynamatics behaviouraly and through neuro-imaging lence.
